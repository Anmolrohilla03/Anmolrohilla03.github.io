Problem Statement

To transform company's global finance data and analytics ecosystem with Data Platform integration and enable faster and actionable financial data services with digital enablement.

Key Challenges

  • Generic site with basic dashboards, some forms and limited cross team collaboration
  • Offline & highly manual business processes via emails and attachments
  • Offline collaboration, majority via phone calls and email
  • Limited understanding of user activity and interactions and personalization of the site
  • Limited reporting, analytics and tracking of KPIs, SLAs and vendor queries
  • Time intensive to search for information on resolving issues related to reconciliation, financial close and other financial processes

Research

User Survey

Conducted user surveys to gain a comprehensive understanding of user needs, behaviors, and pain points at scale. The goal was to identify patterns in user challenges, prioritize features that users found most valuable, and quantify their satisfaction with the current system.

Survey Design

  • Platform Used: Google Forms, Typeform, and SurveyMonkey
  • Number of Participants: Reached out to 50 users, with 38 completing the survey (76% response rate)

Survey Sections

  • Demographics: Role, experience with financial tools, and comfort with AI
  • Current Pain Points: Challenges with existing platforms, such as navigation, task management, or data overload
  • Feature Wishlist: Preferences for new features like AI insights, customized dashboards, or predictive analytics
  • Usability: Ratings on ease of use and satisfaction with the current system

Personalized Experience

I designed a dashboard experience tailored to finance users, integrating key components such as a calendar to manage meetings, report deadlines, and task reminders. The platform incorporates AI-powered insights and alerts, offering personalized recommendations and real-time updates on critical reports. This ensures users stay informed and proactive while fostering seamless cross-team collaboration by centralizing key tasks and shared data within an intuitive interface.

See your day or week at a glance to plan ahead for important tasks

Search across reports, resources, and other integrated systems

Key notifications across System of Engagement are surfaced up front, including AI insights and suggestions

AI Integration

I designed and integrated an AI-driven chatbot to help teams quickly access actionable insights by answering key business questions. This chatbot empowers users to explore trends, identify root causes, and make data-driven decisions without needing advanced analytics expertise.

Here's how it works:

  • The chatbot leverages AI to process large datasets and surface relevant answers in real-time
  • It is tailored to support a wide range of queries related to sales, performance, and customer behavior
  • It enables users to drill deeper into metrics, uncover anomalies, and understand the "why" behind trends

This tool was designed to reduce reliance on analysts for everyday queries, enabling faster decision-making and improving efficiency.

Team Insights

I focused on creating systems that not only enable users to track their progress seamlessly but also provide visibility into team activities and overall workflow efficiency. Integrating capabilities to monitor KPIs, such as the number of tasks left, ensures stakeholders can align priorities effectively while facilitating strategic decision-making.

Collaborate with your team and easily see tasks and status information to ensure smooth deliverable planning

The system recognizes monthly close process is coming up and auto-creates tasks so you can assign owners

Performance Analysis

To address the lack of effective KPI tracking and reporting for finance teams, I designed a streamlined dashboard that provides a clear, at-a-glance view of key metrics and trends. AI-powered insights highlight actionable data and potential business impacts, helping users focus on what matters most.
