Procurement Control Tower: Centralizing Workflows Across Teams

I designed a centralized control tower to unify procurement operations across teams—bringing together KPI dashboards, task tracking, training materials, and reporting tools into a single, scalable interface. This platform was tailored to support cross-functional collaboration, simplify daily workflows, and increase leadership visibility across the procurement pipeline.

Pycube Procurement Control Tower – Landing View

What Wasn't Working and What I Set Out to Solve

Challenge

  • 🧩 Scattered tools across teams (emails, spreadsheets, legacy dashboards)
  • ⏳ Manual delays in reporting & task tracking
  • 🔄 Miscommunication across departments
  • 👁️ Limited visibility for leadership
  • 📂 Training resources hard to access

Goal

  • 🧭 Centralize dashboards, tasks, training, and documentation
  • ⚡ Streamline workflows across procurement teams
  • 🧠 Improve decision-making through visibility
  • 📈 Build a scalable, intuitive, and efficient platform
  • 🔄 Enable faster onboarding and cross-functional adoption

Discovery & Ideation

To ground the design in user needs, I conducted interviews with stakeholders from procurement, operations, and finance. This phase helped identify usability gaps and opened up opportunities to streamline daily workflows across teams.

Research Focus Areas

  • 🎯 Understanding business goals and roles
  • 🧭 Mapping current user flows and common tasks
  • ⚠️ Spotting friction points and inefficiencies
  • 🔍 Auditing underused vs. valuable features
  • 💡 Collecting future-focused feature ideas

Top User Needs Identified

  • ✅ Task management that's flexible and clear
  • 📁 Structured document access
  • 📰 Procurement-specific news and updates
  • 🤖 AI-powered insights to boost decision-making
  • 🎓 In-app training modules for new users
