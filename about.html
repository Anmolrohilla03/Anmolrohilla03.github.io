Hello There!

I once took a class on wireframing and realized it was something that came naturally, and I enjoyed it. Following it, I came across an improper use of layout, color, typography, icons, visual hierarchy on one of the websites and it felt like fingernails across a chalkboard which helped me develop an interest in visual design.

This further led to my curiosity about UX/UI trends and empathy towards studying customer behavior. Hence, I decided to follow my path in designing.

I am a designer with intellectual creativity. I am passionate about working in an interactive environment where fun, creativity, and value form a great team.
